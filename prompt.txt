### 2) Simple Layer (Non-Technical)

**What This Page Does:**
This is your AI assistant that helps you create content and manage tasks by talking to it. It's like having a smart helper that knows about your projects and skills.

**How People Use It:**
1. Type a request like "Draft a social post for my project"
2. Mention specific things using @ (projects) or # (skills)
3. AI creates a draft for you
4. Tap one button to send it to your Content area
5. Or speak your request using the microphone button

**Why It Helps:**
- Saves time creating content from scratch
- Knows about your specific projects (you told it by using @)
- Creates stuff you can actually use (not just ideas)
- Works by voice if you're busy or on the go

## JARGON BRIDGE (TERMS USED ABOVE)

**Entity Mention** - Typing @ or # to reference a project/skill in the AI chat. Creates links between different parts of the app.

**Stagger Animation** - When multiple elements appear one after another with small delays (like cards appearing 0.1s apart). Creates smooth, polished feeling.

**Conversion** - Getting users to do the important action you want (sign up, complete a task, make content, etc.). Good UI makes conversions easy.

**Progress Bar** - The colored bar showing how complete something is (like a loading bar but for tasks/skills/projects).

**Touch Target** - The tappable area around a button or link. Bigger = easier to tap without mistakes.

**Modal/Overlay** - A popup window that appears on top of the current screen (like when you tap @ and see a list of projects).

**FAB (Floating Action Button)** - The round + button that floats at bottom-right corner. Always shows the main action for that screen.

**CTA (Call To Action)** - A button that asks the user to do something ("Apply to Content", "Schedule", etc.).

**Badge** - Small label or number showing status/count (like the "3" next to IDEAS or "AI Generated").

**Horizontal Scroll** - When you swipe left/right to see more items (like the Quick Actions row).

**Filter Tabs** - Buttons at top that show different subsets of content (Active, All, Paused, etc.).

**Micro-Animation** - Tiny animation like a checkmark drawing itself or a card fading out. Makes interactions feel smooth.

**State** - Different versions of how something looks (Normal, Pressed, Disabled, Loading, etc.).

## PRO SUMMARY (3 BULLETS)

- **6-module architecture** with clear separation: command center (Home), execution engines (Projects, Skills, Content), AI assistance layer (Assistant), performance analytics (Performance) - each optimized for specific user job-to-be-done
- **Cross-module entity linking** via @ mentions (projects) and # mentions (skills) creates contextual awareness - AI Assistant generates actionable artifacts that apply directly to Content queue with zero manual data re-entry
- **Gamification through visibility**: progress bars, percentage metrics, time tracking, and trend indicators drive habit formation and completion behaviors - every module surfaces "next action" to reduce decision paralysis

- **Six main sections** accessed via bottom tabs: Home shows today's priorities, Projects tracks your ventures, Skills tracks learning, Content manages creation pipeline, Assistant helps with AI, Performance shows money made/lost
- **Everything connects**: Mention a project in AI chat using @ and it knows the context; generated content moves to your Content queue with one tap; skills link to projects to show what you're learning for what
- **Progress everywhere**: Bars, percentages, and time counters show how you're doing - makes you want to fill up those bars and hit 100% (feels good to see progress)
